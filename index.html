AMBSE Digital Experience Shared Services

Product & Platform Team. Brand Marketing Partner.

Digital Experience is part of the brand marketing ecosystem by enabling the products, platforms, and execution channels brands use to reach fans. Brand teams lead the strategy, message, content, audience decisions, and business outcomes that activate those platforms.

30-second read:
If the work is about the product, platform, vendor, roadmap, QA, compliance, or execution infrastructure, Digital Experience leads. If the work is about what the brand says, who receives it, why it matters, or how success is measured, the brand leads.

Operating Model by Brand

Falcons + United

Mature team model

  • Digital Experience supports product/platform needs.
  • Brands own marketing, content, journey logic, and fan communication.
  • Same service model across both sports teams.

NWSL Atlanta

Build-phase exception

  • Digital Experience temporarily acts as the full digital marketing entity.
  • Ownership shifts to NWSL as the team hires digital roles.

Mercedes-Benz Stadium

Full management model

  • Digital Experience manages stadium digital needs end-to-end.
  • Acts as the engine for stadium digital marketing execution.

We Do

  • Build and govern digital platforms
  • Lead high-end, dynamic web/app experiences for tentpole moments (kit launches, schedule release, draft)
  • Manage vendors, roadmaps, QA, budgets, and platform health
  • Execute approved work through supported channels
  • Lead ADA/accessibility digital content coordination

We Need

  • Complete campaign brief and business objective
  • Target audience, success metric, timing, and CTA
  • Final copy, creative, offer, URL/deep link, and approvals
  • Journey logic, audience rules, and channel strategy
  • Advance planning for larger campaigns and journeys

We Do Not Own

  • Brand-led marketing strategy or campaign concepting
  • Copywriting from scratch, social, PR, photo, or video
  • Offer creation, merch strategy, or ticket sales priorities
  • Final business, legal, sponsorship, or partner approvals
  • Basic/routine web page builds and publishing using the league's core CMS functionality
Operating Principle
Digital Experience operates the ecosystem. Brand teams decide the message, audience, objective, and definition of success.

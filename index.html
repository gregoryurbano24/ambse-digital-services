Digital Experience Owns

  • Digital products, platforms, roadmaps, governance, vendors, budgets, and QA
  • Early platform consultation, data-informed recommendations, channel feasibility, accessibility, compliance, and execution infrastructure
  • Guidance on how brands can maximize apps, websites, email, SMS/RCS, push, chatbot, maps, and lifecycle tools
  • Execution support once the brand plan, priorities, content, audience rules, and approvals are fully defined
Boundary

Brand Teams Own

  • Brand-led marketing strategy, campaign concepts, business objectives, and success metrics
  • The full omnichannel plan, including audience definition, journey logic, offers, promotions, CTAs, copy, creative, and content calendars
  • Organic/paid social, photography, video, PR, ticketing priorities, sponsorship obligations, and approvals
  • Returning to Digital Experience with a complete, prioritized, fan-centric plan for platform execution

Planning Partnership Model

Digital Experience should be involved early, but should not become the owner of the brand plan.

1. Early Platform Framework Brands meet with Digital Experience to discuss platform capabilities, data opportunities, journey ideas, channel fit, and campaign goals.
2. Brand Builds the Plan Brand teams turn the framework into a robust omnichannel plan with messaging, content, audience rules, priorities, approvals, and success measures.
3. Digital Executes + Optimizes Digital Experience reviews the complete plan for feasibility, builds or supports execution, QAs the work, and helps optimize platform performance.

We Do

  • Join early planning to shape platform and data strategy
  • Build and govern digital platforms
  • Manage vendors, roadmaps, QA, budgets, and platform health
  • Execute approved work through supported channels
  • Advise on capabilities, best practices, ADA/accessibility, and optimization

We Need

  • Early inclusion before plans are locked
  • Complete campaign brief and business objective
  • Target audience, success metric, timing, and CTA
  • Final copy, creative, offer, URL/deep link, audience rules, and approvals
  • A fully prioritized omnichannel plan from the brand team

We Do Not Own

  • Brand-led marketing strategy or campaign concepting
  • Full omnichannel plan development for mature brand teams
  • Copywriting from scratch, social, PR, photo, or video
  • Offer creation, merch strategy, or ticket sales priorities
  • Final business, legal, sponsorship, or partner approvals
Operating Principle
Digital Experience helps shape the platform framework early. Brand teams own the full fan-centric plan, then Digital Experience supports execution through the right products, platforms, data, and channels.
